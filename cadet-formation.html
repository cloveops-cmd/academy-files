Formation — squads in ranks

Squad 1 is the front rank. Each squad leader (yellow) is the leftmost position in their rank. Numbers run left → right, so the whole formation reads in number order from the front-left.

Single-file line — fall-in order

The entire class in one line by number. Because numbering runs straight through each squad, every squad is a contiguous block — cadets just count off and peel into their rank when the formation is called.
